The Spirit of Halloween in the Heart of Learning

At Lingua Franca, we believe that language learning should be as fun and engaging as possible! Here, our students have fun celebrating Halloween, dressing up as monsters and imaginary creatures, while practicing their English in playful contexts. Our teaching methods create interactive environments where children learn while having fun, thereby improving their language skills without pressure. Whether playing or participating in events like this, each moment is an opportunity to enhance our students' linguistic abilities while fostering their creativity.

Learning Through Play

Language learning isn't just about reading and writing. At Lingua Franca, we integrate interactive games like these, where children use a whiteboard to participate in stimulating educational games. These activities promote hands-on language learning by encouraging students to communicate in English or Russian in a dynamic and cooperative setting. Our courses are designed to stimulate the mind, make learning more engaging, and allow each child to progress while enjoying actively participating.

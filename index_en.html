Lingua Franca – Language School in Vierzon

Located in the heart of the business incubator in Vierzon, Lingua Franca is a dynamic language school that offers Russian, English, and French courses for all ages and levels. Whether you are a beginner, intermediate, or advanced, our experienced teachers are committed to providing you with high-quality instruction tailored to your needs and goals. We believe in the importance of intercultural communication and offer flexible training, both in-person and online, to enable everyone to progress at their own pace. Join us and discover a stimulating and friendly learning environment!
